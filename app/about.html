Our Story

2010

Founded in Harare

Started as a small agricultural consultancy with just 3 team members focused on improving local farming techniques.

2014

First Expansion

Opened our first regional office in Bulawayo and launched farmer training programs across Matabeleland.

2018

Technology Integration

Developed our first digital platform connecting farmers with markets and launched mobile advisory services.

2023

National Recognition

Awarded "Agricultural Innovator of the Year" by Zimbabwe Agricultural Society for our marketplace platform.

Our Mission

To revolutionize Zimbabwean agriculture through innovative solutions that increase productivity, sustainability, and profitability for farmers at all scales.

Our Vision

A Zimbabwe where agriculture drives economic growth, ensures food security, and provides dignified livelihoods through modern, sustainable practices.
